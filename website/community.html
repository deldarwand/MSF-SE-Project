The Future of MediDroid

In the future, we hope several features can be added to the app to further enhance its utility and experience:

  • Include the possibility to create or edit current POI markers. If a current marker points to a broken bridge on the map, and the user can clearly see the bridge has been fixed, he has the option to edit it. Or, if a broken bridge is discovered, he has the option to create a marker that will be added to the database.
  • The drone can be equipped with a GPS whose coordinates can be sent through the server and used in the application. This could be useful for updating the location on the map relative to the droneâ€™s horizontal movement, and also trace its path.
  • The drone can be equipped with further sensors for different uses: attaching a thermal camera could be used to get an estimate of population. If we can get the area of the field, and the area from the thermal camera that corresponds to a temperature of 37 degrees Celsius (average body temperature), and estimate the area of a single person; then with a mathematical equation we can give an approximation of head counts.
