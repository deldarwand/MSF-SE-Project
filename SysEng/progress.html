Meetings

Meeting 1: Oct. 15th 2015

Attendees: Dr. Dean Mohamedally, Usama Inam (Google), Dr. Kostkova (Client), Aron Monszpart (TA), Team

Highlights: Initial meeting for the project and brief. The project will involve a drone equipped with a camera and several sensors which would send data to the Google Cardboard. The Cardboard view would include the data rendered as graphs, and video/image footage from the drone.

Meeting 2: Oct. 23rd 2015

Attendees: Team, Aron Monszpart (TA)

Highlights: We discussed how to display the data and video streams in Google Cardboard view, and created an initial workflow for the drone system as a diagram. Allocation of tasks amongst the team for the upcoming weeks.

Meeting 3: Oct. 29th 2015

Attendees: Usama (Google), Team, Aron Monszpart (TA)

Highlights: Discussion on hardware: sensors, which ones and how to attach them to the drone. Agreement that the focus for this term should be on rendering the data from sensors, and not live video footage which will be a task for the second phase.

Meeting 4: Nov. 13th 2015

Attendees: Dr. Kostkova, Rosamund Southgate (MSF), Idriss Ait-Bouziad (MSF), Team

Highlights: Meeting with the representatives of MSF who gave their perspective on the project, leading to new requirements. They are interested in population estimation, capturing live video footage and rendering images to create a large aerial map of the area. Suggestions of using heat cameras and infrared sensors, which would have more impact than gas/temperature sensors.

Meeting 5: Nov. 19th 2015

Attendees: Usama (Google), Team, Aron Monszpart (TA)

Highlights: Updating Google with new requirements gathered from MSF and Dr. Kostkova. Agreement that we would continue using the sensors and focus on video/image footage and analysis next term.Demonstration of an early prototype of the Google Cardboard UI.

Meeting 6: Nov. 26th 2015

Attendees: Team, Aron Monszpart (TA)

Highlights: Decisive discussion on how to render graphs in the Google Cardboard, and on transferring data to the phone. Also on analyzing video footage, we should capture an aerial view of a crowd and track individuals.

Meeting 7: Dec. 3rd 2015

Attendees: Team, Aron Monszpart (TA)

Highlights: Update on the project. We went over what we will be able to deliver for the deadline and if it meant the requirements and scope we had agreed on for this phase of the project. Assigned tasks for website and video documentation.
