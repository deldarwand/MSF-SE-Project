Meet MediDroid

The emerging drone technologies give us access to geographical areas that were once hard of access. MediDroid is an application that makes use of drones, enhanced with sensors and cameras, to provide users with data and video footage. With this new perspective, we can give organisations like Medecins Sans Frontieres a new tool to work in critical regions.
